What I do

I’m a Front End UI Developer with a passion for writing clear, polished, code to create the most responsive and accessible experience possible for flawless, user-friendly, design implementations. With a focus on HTML5 and CSS3, I utilize the latest technologies and practices towards responsiveness, browser support, and accessibility for the targeted end-user audience.

My skill-set:

I employ effective means of support through dependable layout methods, as well as the freshest trends for responsiveness and design using tools like Flexbox and CSS Grid. I have a heart for encouraging the most accessible implementations available for limited end-user support, through the simplest code, for efficient load times and image rendering. I aim for reusable and maintainable code using Sass, BEM, and easy-to-understand CSS architectures.

I also aspire towards Full-Stack Application Development using tools like Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, and Passport.js authentication and authorization.

 

Who I am

I spent my childhood on Central Florida’s space coast, in NASA’s backyard, where my parents were always involved with the quickly-evolving tech climate of the 80’s and 90’s. We later moved to the Costa Rican rainforests where I was raised up, homesteading the land, and learning with my father how to help solve the problems of off-grid self-sufficiency for ourselves and many others throughout the country, through creatively developed means for electricity and water access in some of the most secluded areas.

This has given me the perspective to be a free-thinking and curious individual, excited to jump right in and solve problems others often hesitate to tackle. I put my own integrity and work ethic at the forefront of every project, willing to be self-critical and attentive until the problem is solved by the most simple and creative means attainable. I sympathize strongly with those who are less fortunate among us, and strive to use my skills to their betterment. With my lovely wife and three children we have thus enjoyed many noteworthy adventures in our time together, and we don’t expect to slow down any time soon!

… I also enjoy pondering the philosophies and ethics of our lives together, fly fishing, foraging for mushrooms and other wild edibles, song-writing and playing music on anything with strings; big trees, cold nights, warm fires, and a well-brewed pint.
